Our Purpose

Making everyday convenience part of where people live.

At izi Micro Market, our purpose is to make daily life easier by bringing modern self-service convenience directly into condo communities.

We believe residents should have easy access to snacks, drinks, and everyday essentials without needing to leave their building or make an extra trip to the store.

By combining convenience, technology, and thoughtful service, we create a reliable shopping experience that adds value to residential living and improves everyday comfort for the community.
